DOILPHIN

Family and Habitat

Dolphins come in many different species, including common dolphins, spotted dolphins, and killer whales. They inhabit warm waters in oceans around the world, often swimming in nearshore and coastal areas.

Appearance and Characteristics

Streamlined bodies and rounded heads. Skin appears in shades of gray or blue, often with unique patterns or spots. Agile eyes that can freely rotate---easier for them to observe their surroundings.

Behavior

Social animals,often live and travel in groups. Excellent swimmers, frequently leaping and frolicking in the water, showcasing their playful and curious nature. Communicate by emitting various sounds---interacting with each other.
