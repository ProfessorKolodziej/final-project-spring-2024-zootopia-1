Pig

Family and Habitat

Pigs typically live in spacious pigsties and farms. Pigsties provide enough space for pigs to live and move around, ensuring they remain healthy and energetic. On the farm, pigs often form groups with other pigs, enjoying the companionship of living together.

Appearance and Characteristics

Pigs have round bodies and chubby faces, making them look very cute. Their skin is usually pink or black, sometimes with spots. Pigs have highly flexible noses, which they use to smell various scents.

Dietary Habits

Pigs are omnivorous animals, eating a wide variety of foods. They enjoy eating various foods, including grains, fruits, vegetables, and even kitchen scraps. Pigs can consume a lot of different things, making them very effective waste processors.
