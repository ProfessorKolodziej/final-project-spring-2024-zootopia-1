WHALE

Family and Habitat

Many different species of whales, including blue whales, humpback whales, and killer whales. From the Arctic to the Antarctic, and even in tropical waters. Whales are often found roaming in the deep sea, but sometimes they can be seen near the coast.

Appearance and Characteristics

Large bodies and streamlined shapes --- swim effortlessly in the water. Their skin typically appears in shades of gray, blue, or black, sometimes with spots or stripes. Whales have enormous mouths capable of filtering large amounts of water for food.

Behavior

Whales are mammals that need to breathe air, and they typically surface to breathe. Whales are highly intelligent and social animals, often cooperating within groups.
