COW

Family and Habitat

Dairy cows typically live in spacious pastures and farms. Pastures provide dairy cows with ample grassland and a comfortable environment to ensure they remain healthy and vibrant. In the pasture, dairy cows often form close bonds with other cows and farmers.

Appearance and Characteristics

Dairy cows typically have black-and-white spotted coats, making them look very charming. They have round eyes and a pair of nimble ears, appearing adorable. Dairy cows usually have long tails, which they use to swat away flies and other pests.

Dietary Habits

Dairy cows are herbivores, primarily consuming grass and other pasture plants. They use their strong jaws and sharp teeth to chew grass, which is then converted into nutritious milk through their complex digestive system.
