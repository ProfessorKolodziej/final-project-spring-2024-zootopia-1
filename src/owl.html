OWL

Family and Habitat

Can be found in every corner of the globe. From dense forests to vast grasslands, and even in bustling cities, owls can find a place to call home.

Appearance and Characteristics

Large eyes and flat faces--- see better at night. Eyes are forward-facing---perceive depth and see in three dimensions, just like humans. Flexible necks( rotating nearly 360 degrees) --- observe surroundings without moving their bodies.

Dietary Habits

Carnivorous birds.: insects, mice, and other small animals. Fly silently, making almost no sound; and then swoop down suddenly to catch their prey.

Communication Methods

Owl's Call are their main way of communicating and marking their territory. Each species of owl has a unique call---find mates and warn other owls away from their territory.
