CHICKEN

Family and Habitat

Chickens are poultry animals, typically found in farms and rural areas. They enjoy living in secure chicken coops. Chickens are social animals, preferring to live together.

Habitat

Primarily live in the forests, grasslands, and marshes.

Appearance and Characteristics

Soft feathers and short, rounded wings. Displaying a variety of colors, including white, brown, and black. They have a pair of small comb-like feathers on their heads, resembling a crown. Chickens have keen eyesight and hearing, enabling them to perceive their surroundings well.

Diet

Chickens are omnivorous animals, consuming a diverse range of foods. They primarily eat grains, insects, worms, and small plants. Chickens particularly enjoy pecking at grains and insects on the ground.
