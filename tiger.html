TIGER

Family

Tigers belong to the cat family—distant relatives of cats you might have at home!

Habitat

Primarily live in the forests, grasslands, and marshes.

Appearance and Characteristics

Beautiful Orange Fur with Black Stripes—Hide in the Forest. Secretive when Hunting Prey. Sharp Teeth and Claws—Excellent Hunters.

Diet

Carnivores—Eat Meat. They hunt a variety of animals from small deer to large buffalo. Smart and patient when hunting, Approach prey quietly and attack quickly.
